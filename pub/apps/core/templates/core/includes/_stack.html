Our Stack and Approach

Entrepreneurs don’t need to be software engineers—just dangerous enough. Learn a little code and you can test ideas fast, automate tasks, and talk clearly with developers. No-code tools promise speed, but they hit ceilings. The moment you need something unique, costs rise and you’re stuck rebuilding.

That’s why we teach “vibe coding.” A light layer of real code gives you flexibility and room to grow. Our stack makes it simple:

  • Django for backends
  • HTMX for frontend data interactions
  • Alpine.js for ui reactivity
  • Tailwind CSS for design

Once the basics click, AI becomes your multiplier. Prompts get sharper, debugging easier, and simple projects grow into powerful systems. AI isn't a replacement—it's your coding partner, helping you refine and scale with confidence.
